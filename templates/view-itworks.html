Fantastic Flyers: Poster Design for Government Staff

Molly McLeod
  • See all of Molly’s Lessons
  • @mollyampersand
  • molly@codeforamerica.org

At a glance

A fun interactive workshop for government employees who have to make event posters and PSAs

  • Audience: Government staff (or anyone!)
  • Time: 1-3 hours
  • Goals: Give participants the tools to talk about design, think critically about what makes a good poster, and how to get started when designing a poster.

Steps

1. Introductions

introduce yourself, and go around the room and have people say their name, what they know about design, and what they want to learn

2. Poster critique

Show a variety of existing posters, both good examples and bad examples.
  • Start by showing each poster for 3 seconds, and ask what people remember
  • Then look at the poster again. Ask: what’s the main message here? What do you like? What would you do differently?

3. Design principles

a. Color
b. Images
c. Typography
d. Layout

4. Practice poster

a. Brainstorm content ideas.
b. One of our Brainstorm visual frameworks together
c. Take 5-10 minutes to do sketches individually
d. Share!

5. Wrap-up
